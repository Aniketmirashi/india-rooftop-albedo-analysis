# Data

## Google Heat Resilience Dataset

This project uses building-level rooftop albedo data from **Google Heat Resilience**, an initiative by Google Research that provides high-resolution rooftop reflectivity (albedo) data to support urban heat mitigation, climate adaptation, and cool-roof planning.

The dataset contains rooftop-level observations, including:

- Mean rooftop albedo
- Latitude
- Longitude

For this study, the India dataset contains approximately **3.34 million rooftop observations** across eight major Indian cities.

---

## Download the Dataset

The official Google Heat Resilience portal is available at:

**Google Heat Resilience**

https://sites.research.google/gr/heat-resilience/

**India Specific Data available at** : https://storage.googleapis.com/eie-cool-roofs-public/india.zip

From the portal:

1. Open the interactive map.
2. Select the desired city or region.
3. Download the rooftop albedo dataset in CSV format.

For more information about the methodology and data, see:

- Google Heat Resilience Portal
- Google Research Blog: *Expanding our Heat Resilience data to 50+ global cities*
- Nature Communications paper: *Estimating high-resolution albedo for urban applications* :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
